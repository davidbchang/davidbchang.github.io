Machine Learning Engineer@Docugami

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Docugami, a startup that leverages AI to extract insights from documents. I focus on building multimodal deep learning models for processing document layouts and answering questions about unstructured documents. I am also interested in building robust MLOps pipelines and applying data-centric workflows to the ML lifecycle.

Before joining Docugami, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science at the University of Washington, Seattle in 2022. I also served as a research assistant focusing on vision and language methods for social commonsense reasoning.
